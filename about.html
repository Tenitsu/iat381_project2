About

Thank you for visiting our store. We are a small two people company working hard to bring the best shoes to you.

History

It all started as an idea between me and my partner. It was always our dream to open a shoe store. After we finally managed to save up enough money to start one, started to consider where we want to open the store. We decided to open it here and serve the local community because of all the kindness we received. We wanted repay the kindness in some way and thus the birth of Alcor Mizar's Shoe Store.

Our commitment

We wanted to bring the best to the local community and aim to provide only the best. Feel free to contact us. We hope to be of service to you.
